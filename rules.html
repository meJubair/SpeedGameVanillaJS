How to Play

    Game rules:
  • To start playing click on START button.
  • After the game started click on the fishes appearing at different places on the screen.
  • You will gain ONE (1) point for each correct click.

    • Game ends:
  • if you click on a wrong place, the game will be over!
  • You will have 3 chances to miss. If you miss 3 fishes the game will be over!
